The mullet is a trendy haircut that blends a neat low fade on the sides with longer hair at the back, offering a modern twist on the classic mullet. Ideal for oval, square, heart, and diamond face shapes, it enhances symmetry and highlights strong features. It works best with medium to thick hair textures, while round face shapes and very thin hair might not suit it as well.
