Blowout Fade

"Rise above the ordinary with a blowout that speaks boldness."

The blowout taper haircut features a gradual fade on the sides and back that blends seamlessly into longer hair on top. The top hair is styled upwards and outwards, often using a blow dryer and hair products to create volume and a bold, airy look. This haircut exudes a casual, edgy vibe and is perfect for making a statement while maintaining a polished appearance.
