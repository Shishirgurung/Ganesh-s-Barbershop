A buzz cut is a minimalist and low-maintenance haircut achieved by evenly trimming the hair close to the scalp using clippers. It's versatile, sharp, and ideal for men seeking a clean, bold look. This haircut complements oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces, as it highlights strong jawlines and cheekbones. It's also a great choice for those with symmetrical facial features or a receding hairline, offering a timeless and confident style.
