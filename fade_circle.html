Low Fade Circle

"keep it simple, let the fade speak for itself."

A low circle fade is a clean, stylish look where the fade starts just above the ears, curving naturally around the head. It's versatile and works best for people with oval, square, or round face shapes, as it balances facial features and enhances a sharp, polished appearance.
